Electric Eel

Fish

South America

Electric Eels inhabit muddy and stagnant waters in South Ameria. They incapacite their prey by generating an electric shock. They can also manipulate the central nervous system and muscles of their prey using a series of electrical pulses.

Super Power: Electricity Generation
